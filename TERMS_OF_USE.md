# Terms of use draft

Clear Sky Watch is provided as a demo tool for comparing destination weather conditions. It is owned by [Demetra](https://www.pappers.fr/entreprise/demetra-941049322).

## No travel, safety, or financial guarantee

Weather forecasts and historical statistics are uncertain. The extension provides decision-support information only. Users remain responsible for travel decisions.

## Forecast limitations

Real forecasts are available only for a limited future range. For longer trips or future dates outside the forecast range, the extension may show typical same-calendar-month statistics based on historical weather data.

## Third-party services

The extension relies on external geocoding and weather APIs. Availability, accuracy, and terms of those services may change.
